About Us

Shalom Wedding Planners

Shalom Wedding Planners has been transforming dreams into breathtaking realities. With over two decades of experience in the wedding and event industry, we specialize in curating celebrations that are elegant, meaningful, and unforgettable.

Our signature lies in our exquisite décor — where every floral arrangement, shimmering light, luxurious fabric, and intricate detail is thoughtfully designed to reflect your unique love story. From grand royal weddings to intimate destination ceremonies, we blend creativity with precision to create spaces that feel magical yet timeless.
